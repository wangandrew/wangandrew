About

I'm a freshman at the University of Pennsylvania studying Computer Science and Economics. I grew up in Dublin, Ohio, a suburb of Columbus. My interests outside of the classroom include debate and social entrepreneurship.

Organizations

Penn Enactus

Penn Enactus is an organization focused on social entrepreneurship. Members form small groups and create projects that generate revenue while creating social good. I'm currently working on Maia, a project that aims to create a B2B coffee project while teaching local high school students the principles of business.

Penn for Youth Debate

Students invovled with Penn for Youth Debate (PFYD) coach debate at high schools all around Philadelphia, and strive to help students improve their critical thinking and public speaking skills through debate. I coach weekly at the Academy At Palumbo, while also judging local debate tournaments.

Penn Science Accross Ages

Penn Science Accross Ages (PSAA) teaches science to local high school students. I've invovled with the Upward Bound branch of PSAA, where a group of volunteers hosts bimonthly science workshops on Penn's campus. Our mission is to inspire local high school students to enjoy science and to pursur higher education.
